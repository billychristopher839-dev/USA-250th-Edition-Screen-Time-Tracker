📜 How the Republic Works

You are the Government of the United States. Your one sacred duty: keep the nation focused. Each day, report your screen time as hours + minutes. The less you scroll, the more disciplined the Republic — and prosperity grows.

The National Day always advances when you log — high screen time or low, the calendar marches from 1776 toward 2026 and never resets. But each year carries a real piece of American history: stay under 4.5 hours and the nation enjoys that year's triumph; go over and it suffers that year's hardship (indicators fall, streak resets). Reach 250 days and the United States becomes #1 in the world.

🗺️ The 50 States: every 5 logged days admits one new state to your Union — in true historical order, Delaware (1787) first to Hawaii (1959) last — regardless of your screen time. By Day 250, all 50 are unlocked. Tap any glowing state to learn its capital, nickname, and a fun fact.
